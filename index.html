BaitBot

BaitBot - The Discord bot made for you in mind.

BaitBot is a multi-purpose bot designed for the encouragement of activity in small and large discord servers.
It offers fun commands, prefixless replies, moderation tools, a discord-wide economy and more!
BaitBot is produced with pride and intention to make Discord a better place for all of us.

What BaitBot offers you

-kick (Kicks a user)

-ban (Bans a player)

-purge (Deletes many messages)

-secret PASS SECRET (save a secret for your friends to find!)

-see PASS (uncover your secrets!)

-pfp (enlarge the profile picture of another user)

-ping (gets the bot's ping.)

-info (get your server's information!)

-meme to get your daily dose! (NEW)

-toggle (toggle baitbot command types!)

-servertoggle (toggle but for your entire server!)
